💡 Tips for Upload:
💡 Provide a link to your Acting Reel hosted on platforms like YouTube or Google Drive.
💡 On YouTube, it's fine to set your video as "Unlisted".
💡 On Google Drive, ensure the sharing is set to "Anyone with the link".

💡 Tips for Acting Reel:
💡 If you don't have a professional reel yet, feel free to use any footage that best showcases your talent.
💡 Focus on short highlights from different scenes rather than full-length clips.
💡 Arrange your best performances first—length is not as important.

💡 Tips for Upload:
💡 Provide a link to your Resume hosted on platforms like Dropbox or Google Drive.
💡 On Google Drive, set the sharing to "Anyone with the link".

💡 Tips for Resume:
💡 Your resume can include your contact information, physical stats (if applicable), location, background, credits, training, and skills.

💡 Tips for Introduction:
💡 Begin with a friendly greeting and briefly introduce yourself (your name, location, or nationality).
💡 Highlight what makes you unique or what you want to convey to the recipient.
💡 3-4 sentences are enough.
